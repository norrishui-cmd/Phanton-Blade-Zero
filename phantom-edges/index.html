Phantom Edges Guide

Phantom Blade Zero has more than 20 unique Phantom Edges that players can mix and match to support different playstyles.

What Is Officially Confirmed

The official Steam description promises over twenty unique Phantom Edges. It presents them separately from the game's more than 30 weapons, so the two groups should not be counted as one inventory.

What “Mix and Match” Means

S-GAME confirms that Phantom Edges can be combined to suit a playstyle. The official page does not yet specify slot count, resource cost, upgrade tiers, duplicate rules, or whether every tool works with every main weapon.
