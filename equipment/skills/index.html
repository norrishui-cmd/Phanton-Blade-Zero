Phantom Blade Zero Skills

S-GAME confirms skills as one way to customize or progress Soul, while the final skill interface, trees, currencies, respec rules, and named abilities remain unannounced.

Skills versus signature techniques

General skills and signature techniques should be tracked separately. Skills are named as a broad progression reward; signature techniques are specifically associated with weapons obtained from powerful foes in the Steam description.

What cannot be inferred

Trailer attacks do not reveal whether a move is a core combo, weapon technique, unlockable skill, Phantom Edge action, or contextual animation. The database will wait for official terminology and the retail menu hierarchy.

Launch verification

Each skill entry will record prerequisites, unlock cost, input, effect, upgrade levels, compatible weapons, respec behavior, and practical applications. Tested boss or exploration examples will distinguish useful advice from copied menu text.

Source and update status

This page is grounded in the official source. It distinguishes published facts from details awaiting the dedicated State of Play or retail build. Rumors, unofficial names, and trailer-only assumptions are excluded.
