Beings of Higher Power

Official descriptions confirm beings of higher power, occult arts, powerful foes, and inhuman monstrosities in the Phantom World. They do not publish a full hierarchy, faction list, or boss roster.

Confirmed supernatural scope

The Steam page names beings of higher power as part of the world's mysteries, while S-GAME's PlayStation introduction says Soul faces powerful foes and inhuman monstrosities. Kungfupunk deliberately mixes martial arts, machinery, and the occult.

No invented bestiary

Trailer creatures will not receive permanent names, species, ranks, or lore based on appearance alone. Repeated footage may show the same entity, and fan translations may conflict with the final English localization.
