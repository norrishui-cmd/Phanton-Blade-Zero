Augmentation

Augmentation is an officially named element of Phantom Blade Zero's world. Current descriptions do not yet say whether it is a player upgrade system, enemy transformation, machinery, story process, or several of these.

Place in Kungfupunk

The Phantom World blends Chinese kungfu, intricate machinery reminiscent of steampunk, occult arts, and stranger powers. Augmentation fits that fusion, but the official text intentionally avoids specifying its rules or availability to Soul.

Claims not yet supported

No confirmed source establishes cybernetic slots, body-part upgrades, crafting trees, permanent choices, visual customization, or an augmentation currency. Machinery visible on a character is not proof of a player-facing system.

What will make this a system guide

A retail mechanic would need an exact menu term, unlock point, costs, effects, limits, reversibility, and relationship to weapons, armor, artifacts, or skills. Until then, this page accurately tracks the official world concept.

Source and update status

This page is grounded in the official source. It distinguishes published facts from details awaiting the dedicated State of Play or retail build. Rumors, unofficial names, and trailer-only assumptions are excluded.
