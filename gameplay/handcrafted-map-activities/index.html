Exploration design · Checked July 19, 2026

Handcrafted Maps and Activities

S-GAME prioritizes multiple handcrafted maps and diverse activities over a giant continuous world built from repeated assets. Exact activity types and region names remain incomplete.

Confirmed design goal

The studio's original PlayStation introduction explicitly contrasts reasonable-size handcrafted maps with a huge continuity full of repurposed assets. This supports denser authored exploration rather than a claim about total game length.

What counts as a verified activity

An activity page needs an official or retail name, location, activation method, objective, reward, repeatability, and failure or missable conditions. A trailer interaction without those facts remains visual evidence, not a guide URL.

Launch coverage

The maps hub will connect each activity to its nearest checkpoint, quest, boss, item, and route. Spoiler-sensitive objectives will be labeled, and patch-dependent changes will retain a dated revision note.

Source and update status

This page is grounded in the official source. It distinguishes published facts from details awaiting the dedicated State of Play or retail build. Rumors, unofficial names, and trailer-only assumptions are excluded.
