Phantom Blade Zero Semi-Open World

Phantom Blade Zero is officially described as semi-open world: multiple handcrafted maps of reasonable size, populated with diverse activities, rather than one huge continuous map.

What semi-open means here

S-GAME chose several authored maps so locations and activities can remain distinct instead of filling a single enormous space with reused assets. This confirms the broad structure but not the number of regions or their exact boundaries.

Travel questions still open

Official material has not fully documented fast travel, backtracking, hub transitions, locked paths, chapter restrictions, loading boundaries, or when each map becomes available. These are launch-verification tasks, not facts that can be inferred from trailer editing.

How the map database will work

Each verified region page will identify entry requirements, connections, checkpoints, activities, bosses, items, quests, missables, and return restrictions. Region URLs will only publish when an official or retail name is available.

Source and update status

This page is grounded in the official source. It distinguishes published facts from details awaiting the dedicated State of Play or retail build. Rumors, unofficial names, and trailer-only assumptions are excluded.
