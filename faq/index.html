Story & Development

Story & Development FAQ

Who is the protagonist?

The protagonist is Soul, an elite assassin serving The Order. He is framed for the murder of the organization's patriarch and begins a search for the true culprit while facing a fatal condition. Read the related guide.

What does the 66-day premise mean?

Official story material says a mystic healer can delay Soul's death for 66 days. It establishes urgency in the narrative, but S-GAME has not confirmed that players face a continuously ticking real-time gameplay limit. Read the related guide.

Is Phantom Blade Zero connected to Rainblood?

Yes. S-GAME describes Phantom Blade Zero as a spiritual rebirth of the Rainblood series, carrying forward its ideas in a new, larger production. That wording does not by itself confirm a direct sequel timeline. Read the related guide.

What is Kungfupunk?

Kungfupunk is S-GAME's label for the game's blend of Chinese martial arts, wuxia ideas, steampunk-like machinery, occult elements, and a dark fictional world. It describes the creative style rather than a standard genre with fixed rules. Read the related guide.

Is Phantom Blade Zero open world?

S-GAME calls it a semi-open world built from multiple handcrafted maps of reasonable size. That is different from one seamless fully open map and leaves final travel, gating, and route structure to be verified. Read the related guide.

What engine does Phantom Blade Zero use?

Phantom Blade Zero is built with Unreal Engine 5. Engine choice alone does not establish final resolution, frame rate, ray tracing, shader behavior, or PC performance. Read the related guide.

Who is making Phantom Blade Zero?

Phantom Blade Zero is developed by S-GAME. The studio's official introduction ties the project to its Rainblood roots and its long-term goal of presenting martial arts action at a larger scale. Read the related guide.

Is the game inspired by Hong Kong action cinema?
