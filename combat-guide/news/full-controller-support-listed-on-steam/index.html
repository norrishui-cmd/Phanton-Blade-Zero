Full Controller Support Listed for Phantom Blade Zero on Steam

The current Steam listing marks full controller support and recommends that players use a controller.

What Is Confirmed

The feature panel references Xbox and PlayStation controllers plus Steam Input. That confirms broad intent, not identical support for every device. Wireless DualSense haptics, adaptive triggers on PC, button glyph detection, simultaneous mouse input, dead-zone controls, and complete remapping remain retail questions.

What It Means for Players

Combat testing will record the input device and control scheme alongside every timing claim. This matters because buffering, trigger behavior, wireless mode, or Steam Input translation can affect execution. Keyboard-and-mouse support will be tested independently rather than treated as inferior without evidence.

Source and Verification

This update is based on Official Steam feature list. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.

The related combat hub will be revised when S-GAME, PlayStation, Steam, the dedicated State of Play, or the release build supplies a concrete change. Rumors, retailer placeholders, community tags, and edited footage are not treated as final mechanical proof.
