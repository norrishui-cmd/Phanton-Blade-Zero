Phantom Blade Zero Upgrade Materials

A material-routing hub for weapon upgrades, Phantom Edge upgrades, farming locations, and limited resources once final item data is available.

Why Materials Need a Separate Hub

In action RPGs, upgrade materials create high-intent searches because players want to know what to spend, what to save, and where to farm. This page sets that structure without naming materials before they are verified.

Material Page Template

  • Exact material name and rarity.
  • First location and repeatable sources.
  • Weapon or Phantom Edge upgrade use.
  • Whether the material is limited, farmable, or missable.
  • Best route and related map link.

Quality Gate

Do not publish individual material pages until the material name, use, and location are confirmed by the game or official source.
