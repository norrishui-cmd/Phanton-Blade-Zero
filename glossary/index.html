Confirmed and Tracked Terms

SoulThe playable protagonist and accused assassin. The OrderThe faction tied to the story's murder accusation. Phantom WorldThe game's dark wuxia setting. KungfupunkThe style label for martial arts fantasy and dark action. Phantom EdgesSecondary combat tools that pair with primary weapons. Sha-ChiA tracked combat resource term that needs launch verification. GhoststepA movement/combat term to document with verified timing. Brutal MovesA danger-move term that should be tied to boss examples. Killer MovesA combat term that needs exact defensive rules after launch.

Glossary Expansion Rule

New entries should be added only when a term appears in official material, platform listings, tutorials, menus, subtitles, item descriptions, or tested gameplay notes.
