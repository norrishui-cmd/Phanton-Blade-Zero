About Phantom Blade Zero Wiki

An unofficial English guide site built to help players follow Phantom Blade Zero before launch and use verified guides after release.

Editorial Standard

Pre-release pages are written from public information and clearly mark anything that still needs final game verification. Launch guides should be updated from hands-on play, screenshots, patch notes, and reproducible route testing.

Source Policy

Priority goes to official S-GAME pages, platform storefronts, PlayStation Blog posts, official trailers, and direct in-game testing. Rumors, leaks, and unverified social posts should not be treated as guide facts.

Update Policy

High-impact pages such as release date, platforms, combat, bosses, and walkthroughs should show the latest checked date and be refreshed whenever storefronts, trailers, demos, or patches change the known information.
