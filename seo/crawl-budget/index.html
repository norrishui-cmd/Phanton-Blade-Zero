Crawl Budget Plan

A crawl management plan for scaling the site cleanly from seed URLs to large launch coverage.

Current Structure

The site uses a full sitemap plus clustered sitemaps for core, combat, story, technical, and operations pages.

Budget Protection Rules

  • Do not index pages with unverified names or one-paragraph stubs.
  • Keep hub pages strong and internally linked.
  • Use sitemap clusters when URL count grows.
  • Prune duplicate intent before submitting new sitemap batches.

Launch Scaling

When the game launches, publish in batches by cluster: release/platform updates, combat mechanics, weapons, bosses, maps, quests, items, achievements, and settings.
