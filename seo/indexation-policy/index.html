Indexation Policy

A practical rulebook for deciding which Phantom Blade Zero pages should be indexed before and after launch.

Index Now

  • Pages answering a distinct pre-launch query with useful context.
  • Source, release, platform, settings, and search-intent hubs.
  • Templates that explain publish gates and prevent thin content.

Hold or Noindex

  • Individual boss pages before boss names and strategies are verified.
  • Individual weapon, item, material, map, quest, achievement, or ending pages before exact names and uses are known.
  • Pages with only a title and no tested answer.

Merge

Merge pages when two URLs answer the same intent, such as two nearly identical platform questions or a thin guide that belongs inside a parent hub.

Update Trigger

When official or in-game data arrives, update the parent hub first, then publish child URLs only after they meet the template requirements.
