Internal Linking Strategy

A linking model that helps users and crawlers move from broad hubs to verified detail pages without orphan URLs.

Hub to Child Rules

Every child URL should link up to its parent hub, across to at least two sibling pages, and down to verified detail pages when available.

Required Link Patterns

  • Boss pages link to combat, weapons, Phantom Edges, maps, and route planner.
  • Weapon pages link to materials, builds, Phantom Edges, and boss matchups.
  • Quest pages link to maps, missables, endings, rewards, and walkthrough.
  • Settings pages link to platform, combat, accessibility, and controller pages.

Anchor Text

Use descriptive anchors such as "parry guide", "PC performance settings", or "spoiler-free story guide" instead of generic text.
