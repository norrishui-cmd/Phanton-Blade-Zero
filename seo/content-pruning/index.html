Content Pruning Policy

A maintenance policy for keeping the wiki useful as it scales from seed pages to hundreds or thousands of URLs.

Prune or Merge When

  • A page has no distinct search intent.
  • A child page repeats the parent hub without new value.
  • A launch rumor is disproven or replaced by official data.
  • A page cannot be expanded after verification.

Keep and Improve When

  • The page answers a durable player question.
  • The page can be upgraded with official or in-game data.
  • The page has useful internal links and clear next steps.

Workflow

Mark the URL in the inventory, merge useful content into the best hub, update internal links, then remove or noindex the weak URL.
