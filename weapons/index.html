Phantom Blade Zero Weapons

The game includes more than 30 weapons and more than 20 unique Phantom Edges. S-GAME says powerful foes can drop their weapons together with signature techniques.

Confirmed Arsenal Size

The official Steam description promises over thirty weapons. It separately identifies more than twenty Phantom Edges that can be mixed and matched for different playstyles. These figures are minimums, not a final itemized list.

How Weapons Are Acquired

The official description states that defeating powerful foes can grant their weapons and signature techniques. Exact drop conditions, guaranteed rewards, missable weapons, and New Game Plus restrictions still require full-game testing.

Weapons and Phantom Edges Are Different

Main weapons define the core fighting technique. Phantom Edges act as an additional tool set for customization. Until the game labels every item, trailer appearances should not be turned into speculative weapon pages or counted twice.
