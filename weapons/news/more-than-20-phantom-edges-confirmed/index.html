More Than 20 Phantom Edges Confirmed for Phantom Blade Zero

The official Steam page promises more than 20 unique Phantom Edges that players can mix and match around their chosen playstyle.

What Is Confirmed

Phantom Edges are presented separately from the more than 30 weapons, so the two counts should not be merged into one list. The public description does not yet publish every name, effect, resource cost, cooldown, upgrade path, or restriction, and video appearances alone cannot settle the final inventory.

What It Means for Players

The database will track Phantom Edges by verified name, acquisition method, effect, cost, pairing logic, and practical use. Build pages will link to these entries only after combinations are tested, avoiding claims that one flashy trailer setup is a universal best build.
