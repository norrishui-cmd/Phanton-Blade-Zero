Phantom Blade Zero Confirms More Than 30 Weapons

S-GAME's Steam description states that players will have more than 30 weapons to wield.

The number is a confirmed minimum, not a final database count. The store page does not identify every category, individual weapon name, upgrade path, rarity, duplicate rule, stance, or acquisition point. Trailer-visible equipment should not be counted as separate entries without official names.

The weapon hub will create one indexable page per verified weapon after launch, including acquisition source, moves, role, upgrade costs, signature technique, and boss use. Unknown placeholders remain outside the sitemap so the 30-plus promise does not turn into thin or speculative pages.
