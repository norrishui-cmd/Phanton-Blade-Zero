Enemy Signature Techniques Are Part of Weapon Rewards

S-GAME states that defeating powerful foes can grant their weapons and, with them, their signature techniques.

What Is Confirmed

The wording supports playable enemy-derived techniques, but it does not explain whether the move is permanently tied to the weapon, unlocked through mastery, equipped separately, upgraded, or powered by a shared resource. It also does not promise that every enemy move becomes available to Soul.

What It Means for Players

Technique pages will document input, weapon requirement, cost, damage role, recovery, acquisition, and a practical combat example. Names will use the retail localization. Demo-era or community labels will remain aliases only if the final glossary confirms they refer to the same mechanic.

Source and Verification

This update is based on Official Steam description. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.

Next Update

The related weapons hub will be revised when S-GAME, PlayStation, Steam, the dedicated State of Play, or the release build supplies a concrete change. Rumors, retailer placeholders, community tags, and edited footage are not treated as final mechanical proof.
