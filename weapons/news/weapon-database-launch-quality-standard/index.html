How the Weapon Database Will Expand at Launch

The confirmed 30-plus weapon promise creates room for a large database, but URL volume alone is not enough to make useful weapon pages.

What Is Confirmed

A weapon entry must have an exact retail name and at least one concrete answer: where it comes from, what technique it carries, how it is upgraded, or what combat role it serves. Screenshots, platform, patch, and localization will be recorded. Unnamed trailer objects will not become indexable pages.

What It Means for Players

At launch, verified entries will roll out in batches grouped by early accessibility and search demand. The sitemap will include only pages passing the quality gate. Build, boss, material, and route pages will create internal links after the same acquisition and terminology checks pass.

Source and Verification

This update is based on Official arsenal baseline. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.

Next Update

The related weapons hub will be revised when S-GAME, PlayStation, Steam, the dedicated State of Play, or the release build supplies a concrete change. Rumors, retailer placeholders, community tags, and edited footage are not treated as final mechanical proof.
