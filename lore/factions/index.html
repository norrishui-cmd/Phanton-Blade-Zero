Phantom Blade Zero Factions

A faction hub for The Order and any verified story groups, built to avoid inventing faction names before launch.

Current Known Faction Anchor

The Order is the key public faction anchor because Soul is tied to it and accused in relation to its patriarch. Other groups should be added only when official or in-game sources verify them.

Verified Relationship

S-GAME identifies Soul as an elite assassin serving The Order. He is framed for murdering the organization's patriarch and hunted by former comrades while trying to find the conspiracy's mastermind. That establishes affiliation, accusation, and conflict; it does not yet verify every rank, member, allied group, or ending outcome.

Editorial Boundary

New faction pages require an official name or a clear in-game label. Trailer costumes, symbols, or enemy groupings are not enough on their own. After launch, entries will cite dialogue, codex text, quests, or item descriptions and will separate what Soul knows at each story stage from late-game spoilers.
