Phantom World Setting Guide

The Phantom World is the dark wuxia setting for Phantom Blade Zero and the foundation for regions, quests, bosses, and lore pages.

Known World Structure

Public information points to semi-open regions with connected areas, branching routes, and some locations that may be reached later through progression or tools.

URL Growth Use

After launch, each region can support route pages, boss pages, collectible pages, side-quest pages, lore pages, and map checklist pages without inventing content early.

Official World Description

S-GAME says the Phantom World brings together kungfu, machinery, occult arts, and stranger powers. The Steam description mentions shifting realities, echoes of memories, augmentation, higher beings, rumors, and a wider mystery. The 2023 developer post describes a semi-open structure made from multiple handcrafted maps of reasonable size rather than one continuous open world.

What Remains Unknown

Final region names, travel rules, map completion, backtracking limits, weather behavior, and collectible counts have not been fully published. This hub will add a region only after an official label or retail map confirms it, then connect that region to its routes, bosses, quests, items, and lore.
