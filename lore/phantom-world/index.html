Phantom World Setting Guide

The Phantom World is the dark wuxia setting for Phantom Blade Zero and the foundation for regions, quests, bosses, and lore pages.

Known World Structure

Public information points to semi-open regions with connected areas, branching routes, and some locations that may be reached later through progression or tools.

URL Growth Use

After launch, each region can support route pages, boss pages, collectible pages, side-quest pages, lore pages, and map checklist pages without inventing content early.
