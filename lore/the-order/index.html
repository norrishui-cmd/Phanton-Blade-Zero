The Order in Phantom Blade Zero

The Order is the organization tied to Soul's assassination background and the murder accusation that starts the story.

Known Context

Public story information identifies Soul as an elite assassin connected to The Order. The murder of The Order's patriarch is the central accusation that pushes Soul into the conspiracy.

Launch Expansion

This page should become a faction hub with members, regions, questlines, boss ties, ending impact, and spoiler-gated story revelations after release.

Confirmed Story Role

The 2023 PlayStation Blog introduction says Soul is an elite assassin serving an elusive, powerful organization called The Order. He is framed for murdering its patriarch, badly injured in the resulting hunt, and left with a temporary cure that lasts 66 days. Former comrades pursue him while he searches for the mastermind.
