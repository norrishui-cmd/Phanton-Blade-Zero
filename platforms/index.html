Phantom Blade Zero Platforms

Phantom Blade Zero is confirmed for PlayStation 5 and Windows PC. No Xbox or PlayStation 4 version has been announced.

PS5 Features Currently Listed

The PlayStation Store lists one player, DualSense vibration and trigger effects, and Game Help support. Performance modes, resolution targets, download size, and preload timing have not yet been specified on the US product page.

PC Information Currently Listed

Steam confirms Windows PC and lets players wishlist the game. Final system requirements and support for Steam Deck, ultrawide monitors, HDR, frame generation, upscaling, or an unlocked frame rate still require official specifications or testing.
