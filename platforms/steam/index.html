Phantom Blade Zero on Steam

Phantom Blade Zero has an official Windows PC listing on Steam. Players can wishlist it now; final PC specifications and several Steam feature labels remain unpublished.

Official Store Details

The official Steam page identifies S-GAME Studio as developer and S-GAME Publishing as publisher. Its date may appear as October 28 depending on timezone, while PlayStation lists October 29 at 02:00 UTC.

Confirmed Game Information

Steam describes a Windows game built in Unreal Engine 5, inspired by Hong Kong martial-arts cinema. It promises more than 30 weapons, more than 20 unique Phantom Edges, and the ability to obtain powerful enemies' weapons and signature techniques.
