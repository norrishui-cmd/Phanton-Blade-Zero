Quest Guide Template

A spoiler-aware template for side quests, route choices, rewards, and ending-related consequences.

Publish Gate

Publish a quest page only after the quest name, start trigger, and at least one completion path are verified.

Required Sections

  • Quest start location and NPC or trigger.
  • Prerequisites and cutoff points.
  • Step-by-step route with spoiler warnings.
  • Rewards and item links.
  • Ending or faction consequence if verified.
  • Failure conditions and recovery options.

Internal Links

Quest pages should link to maps, route planner, missables, endings, collectibles, and any boss or item pages involved.
