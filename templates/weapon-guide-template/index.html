Weapon Guide Template

A scalable structure for future weapon pages once weapon names and behavior are verified in-game.

Publish Gate

Do not publish an individual weapon URL until the weapon name, acquisition method, and combat role are verified.

Required Sections

  • Where to get the weapon.
  • Best role: beginner, burst, control, mobility, or boss pressure.
  • Core attacks and safe punish strings.
  • Upgrade materials and priority.
  • Best Phantom Edge pairings.
  • Boss matchups and build links.

Ranking Rule

Best-weapon lists must link to evidence-based individual weapon pages after launch; before that, they should remain ranking frameworks.
