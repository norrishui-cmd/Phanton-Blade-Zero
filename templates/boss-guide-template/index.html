Boss Guide Template

A publishing standard for future boss pages so the site can scale without thin or speculative content.

Publish Gate

Publish an indexable boss page only after the boss name, encounter location, and at least one completed strategy pass are verified.

Required Sections

  • Spoiler-free quick tips.
  • Location and route context.
  • Recommended weapon and Phantom Edge if tested.
  • Danger moves, tells, and defensive answers.
  • Punish windows and phase changes.
  • Rewards, quest impact, achievement impact, and rematch notes.

Internal Links

Every boss page must link to the parent boss hub, combat guide, relevant weapon pages, relevant Phantom Edge pages, route planner, and settings/camera page if visibility matters.
