Phantom Blade Zero Achievements and Trophies

A completion hub for achievements, trophies, boss clears, endings, collectibles, difficulty conditions, and missable warnings.

Pre-Launch Policy

Achievement and trophy names should not be guessed. This page exists to prepare the completionist structure and can be expanded once platform lists or in-game achievement data are public.

Completion Guide Structure

  • Roadmap by playthrough count.
  • Missable achievements and point-of-no-return warnings.
  • Boss-related achievements and strategy links.
  • Ending-related achievements with spoiler controls.
  • Collectible and upgrade material checklists.

Why It Deserves a Hub

Achievement users often need a different path than first-time players. They care about efficiency, missables, route order, and irreversible choices, so this board should link tightly to endings, side quests, maps, and New Game Plus.
