90-Day Plan to Reach 2,000 Quality URLs

The goal is not to index 2,000 thin pages today. The goal is to build a verified publishing system that can safely reach 2,000 useful URLs within 90 days as official and in-game data becomes available.

Phase 1: Pre-Launch Authority, Days 1-30

Publish 80-150 pages around confirmed facts: release, platforms, story, Soul, The Order, Phantom World, kungfupunk, combat terms, Phantom Edges, trailers, demo tracking, FAQs, and comparison-intent explainers. Keep speculative pages out of the sitemap until verified.

Phase 2: Launch Capture, Days 31-60

Scale to 600-900 URLs using tested templates: boss pages, weapon pages, Phantom Edge pages, region walkthroughs, side quests, endings, achievements, upgrade materials, settings, performance, and troubleshooting.

Phase 3: Long-Tail Expansion, Days 61-90

Reach 1,500-2,000 URLs through combinations that have real user value: boss-by-weapon strategies, region collectible checklists, quest outcome variants, build matchups, achievement steps, patch pages, and localized question pages.

Quality Gate

  • Every indexed page must answer a distinct query.
  • Every page must link to a parent hub and at least two sibling or child pages.
  • No invented boss names, weapon names, rewards, map labels, or quest outcomes.
  • Use noindex for planned pages until content is verified.
  • Split sitemap files by cluster after 500 URLs.
