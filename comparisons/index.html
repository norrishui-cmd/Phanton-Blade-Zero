Editorial Standard

Comparison Rules

These pages compare confirmed design direction, not marketing vibes. S-GAME describes a fast wuxia action game with a semi-open world, more than 30 weapons, more than 20 Phantom Edges, and simplified execution for elaborate move chains. A PlayStation hands-on preview places it between character-action games and Soulslikes, but that observation does not prove shared progression, checkpoint, death, posture, or difficulty systems.
