Phantom Blade Zero Defense Guide

A comparison hub for block, parry, dodge, Ghoststep, spacing, and boss survival.

Defense as Decision-Making

The best defense guide teaches choice, not button order. Players need to know when to study, when to stand ground, when to move, and when to convert a safe opening.

Decision Framework

  • Block when learning rhythm or surviving unfamiliar strings.
  • Parry when the timing and reward are worth the risk.
  • Dodge when repositioning is safer than contact defense.
  • Use movement tools when boss scale, camera, or area danger changes the problem.

Launch Verification
