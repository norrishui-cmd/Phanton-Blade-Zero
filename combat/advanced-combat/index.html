Pre-Launch Role

This page should remain a framework until exact mechanics are tested. It links players to defense, resources, weapons, Phantom Edges, and bosses.

Verified Foundation

Official material confirms a large arsenal, enemy weapon acquisition, signature techniques, and mix-and-match Phantom Edges. That makes weapon switching and loadout planning legitimate advanced topics, but it does not yet prove animation canceling, combo scaling, hidden invulnerability, or exact meter loops. Those claims will stay out of this guide until they can be reproduced in the release build.

Testing Standard

Each advanced technique should record the platform, patch, input sequence, starting resource state, weapon, target, and repeatable result. Video evidence and a simpler beginner alternative will be added where possible, so a flashy route is never presented as the only viable answer.
