Killer Moves in Phantom Blade Zero

A pre-launch hub for Killer Moves, danger cues, defensive rules, and boss-page documentation standards.

How to Use This Page

Killer Moves should be explained with verified in-game terminology, screenshots, and boss examples after release. Until then, the page acts as a careful term hub.

Boss Guide Integration

Every boss page should list Killer Moves separately from normal attacks so players can scan the safest answer quickly.

Verification Boundary

“Killer Move” is a high-interest demo-era term, but the final game's official public listings do not yet define its cue, input, or defensive rule. No single block, parry, dodge, or Ghoststep answer should be assumed across all enemies. Retail tutorials and accessibility settings will be the primary evidence.

Boss-Guide Standard

After launch, every listed Killer Move must include a plain-language tell, the safest tested response, an alternative for slower reactions, the resulting punish window, and the patch used. Failed responses will also be recorded when useful, because explaining why an intuitive block or early dodge fails is often more valuable than naming a button.
