Phantom Blade Zero Beginner Guide

A spoiler-light starting route for players who want to understand the combat rhythm before chasing perfect boss kills.

First Priorities

  • Learn the difference between blocking, parrying, dodging, and repositioning.
  • Spend resources after confirmed openings instead of during panic moments.
  • Test each weapon against small enemies before judging it in boss fights.
  • Keep spoiler-heavy quest and ending information out of the beginner path.

Early Mistakes to Avoid

Do not treat every red or dramatic animation as a dodge-only problem until final tutorials are verified. The guide should teach observation first, then move choice.

Recommended Next Steps

Read the combat guide, then review weapons and Phantom Edges before opening boss pages.
