Spoiler-Free Phantom Blade Zero Launch Preparation Checklist

Players can prepare for launch without learning bosses, late-game regions, betrayals, or ending conditions.

What Is Confirmed

Before release, confirm the account-region countdown, available storage, controller connection, chosen interface and subtitle language, and accessibility needs. On the first session, record the game version before changing settings, then adjust camera, brightness, subtitles, and controls. Learn one defensive response at a time instead of copying an unverified build.

What It Means for Players

This wiki treats the public setup—Soul, The Order, and the 66-day cure—as the spoiler-free baseline. Detailed character, quest, boss, and ending pages will use warnings. Launch guides will stop before irreversible decisions and link to spoiler-heavy routes only after explicit notice.

Source and Verification

This update is based on Confirmed storefront features. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.

Next Update
