Phantom Blade Zero Is Officially Listed as Single Player

Both major storefronts frame Phantom Blade Zero as a single-player game: PlayStation says one player, and Steam lists the single-player feature.

What Is Confirmed

The campaign follows Soul, a fixed protagonist pursuing the conspiracy behind his ruined heart and 66-day deadline. Current descriptions do not announce online co-op, local co-op, or competitive PvP. An age-rating note about online interactions would not on its own confirm a playable multiplayer mode.

What It Means for Players

The beginner path will focus on solo progression, defensive learning, weapons, Phantom Edges, and story-safe routing. If asynchronous features, leaderboards, messages, or sharing tools appear later, they will be documented separately rather than mislabeled as co-op.

Source and Verification

This update is based on PlayStation and Steam listings. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.
