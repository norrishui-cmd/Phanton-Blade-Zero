Phantom Blade Zero Lists 15 Supported Languages

The Steam language table lists 15 interface and subtitle languages. English and Simplified Chinese are the two rows currently marked for full audio.

What Is Confirmed

German, French, Spanish, Russian, Japanese, Traditional Chinese, Brazilian Portuguese, Korean, Italian, Thai, Vietnamese, Portuguese from Portugal, and Latin American Spanish are listed for interface text and subtitles. A localized store page does not itself prove a full dub.

What It Means for Players

New players can choose a supported text language with more confidence, but independent voice-and-text selection is not yet confirmed. The settings guide will test subtitle size, background, dialogue logs, language-pack downloads, mid-save changes, and whether PS5 and PC offer the same choices.

Source and Verification

This update is based on Official Steam language table. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.

Next Update

The related beginner hub will be revised when S-GAME, PlayStation, Steam, the dedicated State of Play, or the release build supplies a concrete change. Rumors, retailer placeholders, community tags, and edited footage are not treated as final mechanical proof.
