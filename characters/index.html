Phantom Blade Zero Characters

Soul is the only playable, named character fully established in the current official store descriptions. This hub separates named characters from roles that remain deliberately unnamed.

Soul

Soul is an elite assassin formerly serving The Order. He is framed for the patriarch's murder, gravely injured during the manhunt, and kept alive by a temporary cure that lasts 66 days. His search for the mastermind drives the campaign.

Confirmed but currently unnamed roles

Official story material identifies The Order's murdered patriarch and a mystic healer who saves Soul, but does not provide reliable English names for either. They remain entries on this hub rather than thin character URLs until an official localization supplies names and meaningful biographies.

Character-page quality rule

A character receives an indexable URL only after a verified name, role, relationship, and source are available. Trailer nicknames, fan translations, visual guesses, and repeated shots do not establish separate characters. Retail codex entries, dialogue, official profiles, and credits will drive the launch database.

Source and update status

This page is grounded in the official source. It distinguishes published facts from details awaiting the dedicated State of Play or retail build. Rumors, unofficial names, and trailer-only assumptions are excluded.
