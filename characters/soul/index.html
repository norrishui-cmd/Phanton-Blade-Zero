Soul in Phantom Blade Zero

Soul is the playable protagonist, an elite assassin connected to The Order who is framed for its patriarch's murder and given only 66 days to live.

Soul's Confirmed Background

In S-GAME's official story introduction, Soul serves a powerful organization called The Order. After he is accused of killing its leader, he is gravely injured during the manhunt.

Why He Has 66 Days

A mystic healer saves Soul, but the temporary cure lasts only 66 days. His campaign follows the search for the real mastermind while assassins and inhuman enemies pursue him. Official material has not confirmed a literal real-time gameplay timer.

Playable Character Status

PlayStation and Steam descriptions both identify Soul as the character the player controls. No custom-character creator has been announced. Weapons and Phantom Edges provide the clearest confirmed form of playstyle customization.

What Remains Spoiler-Gated
