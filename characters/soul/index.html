Character

Soul in Phantom Blade Zero

Soul is the playable protagonist: an elite assassin framed for murder and forced into a race against a 66-day countdown.

Confirmed Role

Soul serves The Order before being accused of killing its patriarch. The temporary cure that saves him gives the story its 66-day pressure, which makes Soul a natural hub for story, combat, and ending pages.
