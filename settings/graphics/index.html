Phantom Blade Zero Graphics Settings

A PC graphics settings page for presets, resolution, FPS, upscaling, HDR, ultrawide, motion blur, and visual clarity testing.

Current Status

Final graphics options must be verified from the launch build. Until then, this page documents what should be tested and how settings advice should be written.

Testing Plan

  • Preset differences and most expensive options.
  • Resolution scaling, upscaling, frame generation, or HDR if present.
  • Motion blur and camera clarity for boss fights.
  • Frame pacing during fast combat and cutscene transitions.
  • Recommended settings by hardware tier after benchmark data exists.
