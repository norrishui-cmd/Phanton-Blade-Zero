Phantom Blade Zero HUD Settings

A HUD and UI readability page for combat clarity, subtitles, resource displays, and accessibility.

What to Verify

  • HUD size and opacity options.
  • Health, resource, and Phantom Edge display behavior.
  • Subtitle placement and readability.
  • Damage number or combat feedback settings if present.
  • Whether the HUD can be hidden or customized.

Why HUD Matters

Action RPG combat depends on readable feedback. HUD advice should connect to resource management, accessibility, and boss visibility.
