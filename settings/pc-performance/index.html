Phantom Blade Zero PC Performance Settings

A non-speculative PC settings page for what to test at launch: graphics presets, FPS stability, input latency, resolution, ultrawide, stutter, and controller support.

What Can Be Said Now

Before final system requirements and launch builds are verified, this page should not claim exact best settings. It should explain the testing plan and prepare the questions PC players will search.

Launch Benchmark Checklist

  • Official minimum and recommended specs.
  • Graphics presets and the heaviest options.
  • Frame pacing and stutter during boss fights.
  • Keyboard and mouse behavior versus controller.
  • Ultrawide, HDR, upscaling, and frame cap support if present.
