Phantom Blade Zero Language and Subtitles

A language and subtitle tracking page for interface languages, voice options, subtitle readability, and accessibility.

What to Verify

  • Interface language list by platform.
  • Voice language options if listed.
  • Subtitle size, background, and speaker labels.
  • Whether language can be changed in-game or only by platform settings.
  • Any regional differences in store listings.

Why This Page Matters

Language support affects buying decisions, accessibility, and guide audience. It should be verified through official storefronts and final menus.

Verified Language Table

Steam currently lists 15 interface and subtitle languages, with full audio for English and Simplified Chinese. See the complete language table for the exact breakdown and checked date.
