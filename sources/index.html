Sources and Verification Policy

The wiki separates confirmed public information, tracked pre-launch questions, and launch-only details that require direct verification.

Preferred Sources

  • Official S-GAME and Phantom Blade Zero pages.
  • Platform store pages for PC and PlayStation details.
  • Official trailers, gameplay demos, and publisher posts.
  • In-game menus, tutorials, item descriptions, and patch notes after release.

What Not to Publish as Fact

Do not invent boss names, weapons, Phantom Edges, quest outcomes, endings, map region names, item names, achievements, system requirements, or performance settings. Those pages should remain hubs until official or tested data exists.

Update Workflow

When a source changes, update the affected hub, add a note to updates, refresh the sitemap lastmod date, and keep old assumptions out of the copy.
