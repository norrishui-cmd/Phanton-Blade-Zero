Phantom Blade Zero Bosses Guide

Boss pages should answer the urgent questions first: what is dangerous, how to survive it, when to punish, and what reward or quest flag follows.

Boss Page Formula

  • Recommended weapon and Phantom Edge.
  • Spoiler-free quick tips at the top.
  • Danger moves with readable tells.
  • Phase changes and safe punish windows.
  • Rewards, achievements, and quest consequences.
