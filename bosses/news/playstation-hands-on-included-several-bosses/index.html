PlayStation Hands-On Reported Several Phantom Blade Zero Boss Fights

The 2024 PlayStation report says its writer played a few levels and fought a few bosses, describing the combat as fluid and visually faithful to the trailers.

What Is Confirmed

The report supports the existence of multiple playable boss encounters in the preview build and highlights blocking, parrying, dodging, and counters. It does not publish a complete retail roster, phase list, rewards, health values, difficulty settings, or final encounter order.

What It Means for Players

The boss hub can discuss the combat-learning role of bosses, but named strategy pages require official names or retail verification. Preview-build behavior will be labeled separately if it differs from launch. A boss count will not be estimated from montage cuts or repeated footage.

Source and Verification

This update is based on PlayStation hands-on report. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.

Next Update

The related bosses hub will be revised when S-GAME, PlayStation, Steam, the dedicated State of Play, or the release build supplies a concrete change. Rumors, retailer placeholders, community tags, and edited footage are not treated as final mechanical proof.
