Can You Use Enemy Signature Techniques in Phantom Blade Zero?

Yes, for obtainable enemy weapons. S-GAME says defeating powerful foes grants their weapons and signature techniques.

How the Official Description Frames It

The feature connects enemy victories to a growing combat arsenal: obtain a powerful foe's weapon and gain its signature technique. That supports build variety, but does not confirm that every boss or ordinary enemy contributes a unique move.
