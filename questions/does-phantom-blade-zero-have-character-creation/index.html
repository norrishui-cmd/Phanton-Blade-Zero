Does Phantom Blade Zero Have Character Creation?

No custom-character creator has been announced. Official descriptions place players in the role of Soul, a named protagonist with a defined history and mission.

A Fixed Story Protagonist

The official PlayStation description says “You play as Soul,” while S-GAME's story introduction identifies him as an elite assassin connected to The Order. That is different from creating an unnamed avatar.

Customization Is a Separate Question

A fixed protagonist can still have outfits, armor appearances, hairstyles, weapon skins, or other cosmetics. S-GAME has not yet published a complete customization feature list, so the absence of a character creator should not be read as proof that Soul has no visual options.

What Can Be Customized?

The officially described combat customization comes from an arsenal of more than 30 weapons and more than 20 Phantom Edges. Exact skill trees, stat allocation, costumes, dyes, and transmog systems still need official confirmation or launch testing.
