Does Phantom Blade Zero Have Multiplayer?

No multiplayer mode has been announced. The official PlayStation Store currently labels Phantom Blade Zero as a one-player PS5 game.

Co-op and PvP Status

The official PlayStation listing shows “1 player.” It does not list online co-op, local co-op, competitive multiplayer, or PvP. The official Steam page also focuses on the story campaign and combat arsenal rather than multiplayer features.

Does One Player Mean Offline?

Not necessarily. A one-player label confirms the supported player count on the current PS5 listing, but it does not by itself confirm offline requirements, network-dependent features, leaderboards, or future challenge modes. Those details need final store labels or launch testing.

Could Multiplayer Be Added Later?

S-GAME has not announced that plan. If a later update adds an online feature, this page will record its platform, player count, subscription requirements, and version date. Until then, buy the game for its single-player campaign rather than an expected co-op mode.
