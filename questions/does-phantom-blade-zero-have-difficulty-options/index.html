Does Phantom Blade Zero Have Difficulty Options?

A difficulty-options tracking page for mode names, assist settings, achievement impact, and accessibility.

Short Answer

Exact difficulty options should be verified from final menus. Do not assume mode names or restrictions before launch.

What to Verify

  • Difficulty names and descriptions.
  • Whether difficulty can be changed mid-playthrough.
  • Whether easier or harder modes affect achievements.
  • Whether accessibility assists are separate from difficulty.

Where to Continue

Use the difficulty guide, accessibility page, and beginner mistakes page for context.

Verification Checklist

No final difficulty menu has been detailed on the official PlayStation or Steam pages. The release build must confirm mode names, unlock conditions, whether settings can change mid-run, and any trophy, achievement, reward, or New Game Plus restrictions. Until then, descriptions based on demos remain provisional.

The guide will also distinguish difficulty selection from accessibility assists.
