Is Phantom Blade Zero on PS4?

No PS4 edition has been announced. The official PlayStation Store identifies Phantom Blade Zero as a PS5 version, so PS4 owners should not expect cross-generation support unless S-GAME says otherwise.

Why the PS5 Label Matters

The official PlayStation Store page lists a PS5 version and names PS5 features including DualSense vibration and trigger effects. It does not list a PS4 product or PS4-to-PS5 upgrade path.

Can PS4 Play the PC Version?

No. The Windows PC release is a separate version available through Steam and cannot run on PS4. Players without a PS5 will need a compatible PC or must wait for an official announcement of another platform.

What to Check Before Buying

Confirm that the store page says “PS5 Version” and that your console account region matches the product you plan to buy. See the PS5 guide for current features and the release page for launch timing.
