Can You Change Difficulty in Phantom Blade Zero?

A difficulty-change tracking page for players deciding how risky their first playthrough should be.

Short Answer

Difficulty names, mid-game change rules, and achievement restrictions should be verified from final menus. Do not assume the answer before launch.

What to Verify

  • Difficulty mode names.
  • Whether difficulty can be changed after starting.
  • Whether changes affect achievements or endings.
  • Whether New Game Plus changes difficulty options.

Why the Answer Is Pending

Official store pages do not currently list selectable difficulty names or whether they can be changed after starting. Event demos may use settings that differ from retail. At launch, this page will test menu availability, mid-game switching, trophy or achievement effects, accessibility assists, and whether boss behavior or rewards change.

Platform differences will be documented separately if the menus do not match.
