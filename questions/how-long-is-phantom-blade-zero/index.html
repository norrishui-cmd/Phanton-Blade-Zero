How Long Is Phantom Blade Zero?

A pre-launch time-to-beat page for story length, side content, completion, and New Game Plus expectations.

Short Answer

The final playtime should not be guessed before release. A reliable answer needs completion data from the final game: main story route, side quests, optional bosses, collectibles, endings, and New Game Plus.

What to Measure at Launch

  • Main story only.
  • Main story plus important side quests.
  • All bosses and major optional content.
  • Collectibles and achievements.
  • New Game Plus or challenge-route time.

Why This Page Matters

Length questions are high-intent for buying decisions and planning. This page is useful now because it explains what will be measured and where players should look next.

Why 66 Days Is Not a Length Estimate

Soul's 66-day deadline is part of the official story premise, not a confirmed real-time campaign timer or hour count. S-GAME has not published main-story, completionist, or speedrun estimates. Reliable figures require multiple completed retail playthroughs with route, difficulty, exploration, and cutscene choices recorded.
