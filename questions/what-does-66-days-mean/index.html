What Does the 66-Day Limit Mean?

Soul has only 66 days left to live after a mystic healer gives him a temporary cure. He must uncover the conspiracy against him before that time runs out.

The Confirmed Story Setup

S-GAME's official introduction says Soul is framed for the murder of The Order's patriarch and badly injured during the resulting hunt. A healer saves him, but the cure lasts only 66 days.

Is It a Real-Time Gameplay Timer?

That has not been confirmed. The number creates narrative urgency, but official descriptions do not state that players lose progress after 66 real hours, in-game days, rests, or missions. Do not plan a speedrun around an unannounced timer.

Does It Affect Endings?

S-GAME has not detailed ending conditions, time-based choices, or whether the number changes through quests. Those mechanics require full-game evidence and spoiler controls.

Why the Number Matters

The countdown connects Soul's personal survival to his investigation and gives the campaign a clear deadline. Continue with Soul's profile or the spoiler-free story hub.
