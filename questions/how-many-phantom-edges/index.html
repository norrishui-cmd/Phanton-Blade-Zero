How Many Phantom Edges Are There?

Phantom Blade Zero has more than 20 unique Phantom Edges according to its official Steam description. The publisher has not yet released a complete named list.

What Is Confirmed

The official Steam listing describes more than twenty Phantom Edges that players can mix and match to suit a playstyle. That confirms the scale of the system but not every name, effect, slot rule, or upgrade requirement.

Why the Final Count May Differ

“More than 20” is a floor rather than an exact total. Trailer tools, enemy techniques, and main weapons should not be counted as Phantom Edges unless the game or publisher labels them that way.

How We Will Catalogue Them

Each verified entry in the Phantom Edge database will include its acquisition route, effect, resource cost, useful pairings, and matchups. Combination pages will appear under builds only when they answer a distinct playstyle or boss problem.
