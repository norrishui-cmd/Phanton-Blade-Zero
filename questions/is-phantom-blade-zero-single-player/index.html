Is Phantom Blade Zero Single Player?

Yes. Phantom Blade Zero is currently listed as a one-player game, with a campaign centered on the assassin Soul and his 66-day story premise.

Official Player Count

The official PlayStation page lists one player. S-GAME's description says the player controls Soul, fights assassins, and uncovers a conspiracy, supporting a story-driven single-player structure.

What the Campaign Includes

Official material confirms fast martial-arts combat, a dark semi-open world, more than 30 weapons, and more than 20 Phantom Edges. Exact chapter count, mission structure, save system, and campaign length remain unconfirmed.

Can It Be Played Offline?

The current one-player label does not automatically prove that every feature works offline. The final store page or launch testing must confirm whether initial activation, patches, cloud saves, or any optional service requires a connection.

Multiplayer Status

No co-op or PvP mode has been announced. See the dedicated multiplayer answer for the difference between a one-player listing and confirmed offline support.
