Does Phantom Blade Zero Have Photo Mode?

A tracking page for photo mode, camera tools, capture options, and platform sharing features.

Short Answer

Photo mode should be treated as unconfirmed until official materials, store listings, or final menus verify it.

What to Verify

  • Dedicated photo mode menu.
  • Camera movement, filters, depth of field, and hide-HUD options.
  • Platform screenshot and capture support.
  • Whether photo mode is available during combat, cutscenes, or only exploration.

Related Pages

See camera settings, graphics settings, and platform pages for capture-related details.

Current Evidence

No photo mode is advertised on the official PlayStation or Steam listings. That does not prove the feature is absent from the final menus or a later update. Launch verification will check camera controls, filters, hiding the HUD, combat restrictions, resolution, and platform differences.

Any platform-specific capture tools will be described separately from an in-game mode.
