Is Phantom Blade Zero a PS5 Exclusive?

Phantom Blade Zero is not PS5-only. It has official listings for both PlayStation 5 and Windows PC. PS5 is currently the only announced console version.

PS5 and PC Are Confirmed

The official PlayStation page confirms the PS5 edition, and the official Steam listing confirms Windows PC. That means “PS5 exclusive” is misleading if it implies the game cannot be played on PC.

Is It a Console Exclusive?

PS5 is the only console announced as of this page's verification date, but the official pages reviewed here do not establish a permanent exclusivity period. It is safer to say “announced for PS5 and PC” than to assume an Xbox version will or will not arrive later.

Choose the Right Version

Console players can review the PS5 features. PC players should follow the Steam page and wait for final system requirements before buying hardware.
