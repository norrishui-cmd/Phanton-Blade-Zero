What Engine Does Phantom Blade Zero Use?

Phantom Blade Zero uses Unreal Engine 5. S-GAME highlights the engine alongside modern motion capture and martial-arts choreography.

Official Confirmation

Both the PlayStation description and Steam page identify Unreal Engine 5. Steam also emphasizes motion capture used to recreate martial-arts techniques.

What the Engine Tells Us

It identifies the underlying technology and helps explain the game's detailed environments, animation pipeline, and current-generation presentation. It does not confirm which Unreal Engine features are enabled in the shipping build.

What It Does Not Confirm

Unreal Engine 5 alone cannot determine frame rate, shader compilation behavior, ray tracing, Lumen, Nanite, upscaling, storage needs, or Steam Deck performance. Those depend on implementation and optimization.

PC Buying Advice

Ignore “estimated requirements” presented as official. Wait for S-GAME or Steam to publish minimum and recommended specifications, then compare them with the PC requirements guide.
