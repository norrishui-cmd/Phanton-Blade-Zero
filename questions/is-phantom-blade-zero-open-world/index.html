Is Phantom Blade Zero Open World?

Current public information is better described as semi-open regions rather than a guaranteed fully open world.

Short Answer

Before launch, the most accurate wording is that Phantom Blade Zero uses connected, semi-open areas with exploration, optional routes, and side content. A final answer needs hands-on verification of region boundaries, fast travel, locked paths, and return routes.
