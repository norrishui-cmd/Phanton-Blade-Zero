How Many Weapons Are in Phantom Blade Zero?

More than 30 weapons are planned, alongside more than 20 unique Phantom Edges. These are publisher-provided minimum counts, not a complete launch inventory.

What the Official Listing Says

The official Steam page says players can wield over thirty weapons and mix and match more than twenty Phantom Edges. It also explains that defeating powerful foes can award their weapons and signature techniques.

Weapons vs. Phantom Edges

Weapons form the main arsenal and carry fighting techniques. Phantom Edges are a separate group of tools that can be combined with the player's chosen style. The exact categories, upgrade paths, and duplicate rules still need in-game verification.

What the Wiki Will Track

The weapon database will list acquisition source, moves, role, upgrades, and boss matchups for each verified weapon. The Phantom Edge hub will cover effects and pairings without counting unconfirmed trailer appearances as separate items.
