Is Phantom Blade Zero on PC?

Yes. Phantom Blade Zero has an official Windows PC page on Steam and is scheduled alongside the PlayStation 5 version in October 2026.

Official PC Storefront

The official Steam page identifies S-GAME Studio as developer and S-GAME Publishing as publisher. Players can wishlist the game now; the page is not yet selling a PC edition as of this guide's check.

Release Date Display

Steam may show October 28 because store dates are localized, while the PlayStation listing gives October 29 at 02:00 UTC. See the release guide before assuming the date represents a separate PC launch.
