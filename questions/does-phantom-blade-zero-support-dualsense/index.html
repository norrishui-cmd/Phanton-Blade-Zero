Does Phantom Blade Zero Support DualSense?

Yes. The official PlayStation Store confirms DualSense wireless-controller vibration and trigger-effect support for Phantom Blade Zero.

What Is Officially Listed?

The PS5 product page explicitly lists vibration and trigger effects. These are confirmed feature labels, unlike performance modes or detailed controller settings that are not yet shown.

What Could Trigger Effects Be Used For?

The store does not explain the exact implementation. It would be speculation to assign effects to specific weapons, parry timing, Phantom Edges, or ranged tools before S-GAME describes them or the retail build is tested.

Haptics and Vibration

The listing confirms vibration but does not provide a scene-by-scene breakdown or separately label advanced haptic feedback. Launch testing should document whether different attacks, surfaces, impacts, and enemy cues feel distinct.

Controller Settings Still Needed

Button remapping, dead-zone controls, vibration intensity, trigger-effect strength, motion input, and accessibility toggles remain unconfirmed. See the PS5 guide for the complete verified feature table.
