Phantom Blade Zero Collectibles Guide

A launch-ready hub for collectibles, map checklists, completion routing, and missable tracking without inventing item names before release.

What This Page Can Say Now

Collectible names, counts, and rewards need final game verification. Until then, this page defines the structure players will need: region checklists, route order, missable warnings, achievement ties, and item-use notes.

Launch Checklist

  • Exact collectible names and categories.
  • Region and checkpoint where each item appears.
  • Whether the collectible is missable or can be collected later.
  • Reward, achievement, quest, or upgrade connection.
  • Spoiler-free route notes for first-time players.

Expansion Plan

After launch, each verified region can support its own collectible checklist. Individual collectible URLs should be indexed only when they contain location, use, screenshots or notes, and related links.
