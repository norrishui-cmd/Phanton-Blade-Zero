Phantom Blade Zero Beginner Mistakes

A spoiler-light guide to the habits that usually hurt players in fast action RPG combat.

Mistakes to Avoid

  • Panic dodging before reading the full attack string.
  • Trying high-commitment attacks before confirming punish windows.
  • Ignoring camera and controller settings.
  • Spending resources because they are available, not because the opening is real.
  • Reading spoiler-heavy ending or quest pages before learning the basics.

Launch Update Plan

After release, this page should add exact tutorial terms, early boss examples, and first-region mistakes without spoiling late-game content.
