Phantom Blade Zero Launch Checklist

A practical checklist for players and guide editors on launch day: verify release timing, platform setup, settings, combat terms, and spoiler controls.

Player Checklist

  • Confirm release time and platform availability.
  • Install updates before starting serious route notes.
  • Set subtitles, camera, brightness, and controller options.
  • Use spoiler-free pages until ready for story and ending details.
  • Bookmark combat, weapons, Phantom Edges, bosses, and maps.

Editor Checklist

  • Capture exact in-game terminology.
  • Record menu names, difficulty names, and platform settings.
  • Do not publish boss, weapon, item, or ending pages until names are verified.
  • Update sitemap only when pages are substantive.

Release Facts to Recheck

Phantom Blade Zero is currently scheduled for October 29, 2026 on PS5, while Steam displays October 28 because of the global UTC unlock. Confirm the storefront countdown in your region rather than assuming a local midnight release. Download size, preload, day-one patch, editions, price, and review embargo remain time-sensitive.

First Evidence Capture

Save the version number, platform, default difficulty, controls, accessibility settings, and tutorial terminology before changing options. For every discovered weapon or Phantom Edge, record the exact name and acquisition source. This creates reliable guide inputs and prevents placeholder terms from becoming permanent database errors.
