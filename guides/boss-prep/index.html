Phantom Blade Zero Boss Prep Guide

A spoiler-light checklist for preparing for hard fights without needing the exact boss name or full strategy.

Before Entering a Boss Fight

  • Confirm your preferred defensive response: block, parry, dodge, or movement.
  • Test whether your weapon can punish quickly after short openings.
  • Save high-commitment moves until you understand recovery windows.
  • Check healing, resource, and Phantom Edge availability.
  • Adjust camera and lock-on settings if the fight is large or fast.

What Boss Pages Should Add

Full boss pages should add verified telegraphs, phase changes, punish windows, rewards, and checkpoint notes. This prep page remains useful for players avoiding spoilers.
