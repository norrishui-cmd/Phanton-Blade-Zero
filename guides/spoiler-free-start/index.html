Phantom Blade Zero Spoiler-Free Start Guide

A clean first-session checklist for players who want combat help without story, boss, or ending spoilers.

First Session Priorities

  • Set camera, subtitles, brightness, and controller comfort first.
  • Learn defense choices before chasing high-damage strings.
  • Test every weapon or Phantom Edge the game gives you before choosing a favorite.
  • Do not open ending or side quest pages until you are ready for spoilers.

What This Page Avoids

This page should avoid boss names, region spoilers, ending triggers, and major quest outcomes. It should link to spoiler-heavy pages only after clear warning.

Confirmed Story Boundary

The public premise says Soul was framed for killing The Order's patriarch, survived through a temporary cure, and has 66 days to uncover the conspiracy. This guide treats those marketing facts as the spoiler-free baseline and will not reveal later identities, betrayals, bosses, regions, or endings.

Safe Navigation

Follow settings, controls, basic defense, and weapon-system pages first. Character, quest, ending, and boss pages may contain names visible in headings, so use their spoiler labels. The release version of this route will stop before the first major irreversible choice and provide a separate warning before any missable content discussion.
