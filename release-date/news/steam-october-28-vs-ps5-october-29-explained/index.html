Why Steam Shows October 28 While PS5 Shows October 29

The current Steam page displays October 28, 2026, while PlayStation displays October 29 at 02:00 UTC. This does not automatically mean PC receives the game a day early.

What Is Confirmed

A single worldwide unlock can fall on different calendar dates depending on region. At 02:00 UTC, parts of North America are still on October 28, while Europe and Asia are already on October 29. Store localization can therefore produce two visible dates for the same moment.

What It Means for Players

The reliable launch check is the countdown inside the account region where the game will be purchased. The site will not describe the Steam version as early access unless S-GAME explicitly announces separate platform timing. Regional release tables will retain UTC alongside local time to avoid calendar confusion.

Source and Verification

This update is based on Steam and PlayStation storefronts. It separates confirmed statements from inferences and records the publication date so later announcements or retail testing can replace outdated information.
