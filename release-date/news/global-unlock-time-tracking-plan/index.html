How the Wiki Will Track Phantom Blade Zero Global Unlock Times

The current PlayStation page provides a useful anchor of October 29 at 02:00 UTC, but regional storefront countdowns still need to be checked near launch.

What Is Confirmed

A timezone table should convert one confirmed instant rather than mixing local-midnight assumptions from different stores. Daylight-saving rules also matter: a static abbreviation can be wrong if the region changes clocks before release. The tracker will therefore show city, UTC offset, local date, and verification status.

What It Means for Players

During launch week, the page will be refreshed from official countdowns and marked if PS5 and Steam differ. Screenshots, check times, and source links will be recorded. Players should still trust the countdown inside their own account region when it conflicts with a general table.
