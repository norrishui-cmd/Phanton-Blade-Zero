Phantom Blade Zero Release Date Is October 29, 2026

PlayStation currently lists Phantom Blade Zero for October 29, 2026 at 02:00 UTC, giving the game a concrete global launch target on PS5.

What Is Confirmed

The listing also confirms a one-player PS5 version, DualSense vibration and trigger effects, and Game Help. It does not yet publish download size, preload timing, editions, price, review embargo, or final performance modes. Those details remain separate from the date confirmation and should not be inferred from retailer placeholders.

Players can safely plan around the October 29 window, while checking their regional storefront countdown before launch. This wiki will update the release hub, timezone page, homepage countdown, schema, and sitemap together if S-GAME or a platform holder changes the official timing.
