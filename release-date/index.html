Phantom Blade Zero Release Date

Phantom Blade Zero releases on October 29, 2026 for PlayStation 5 and Windows PC. The US PlayStation Store currently shows an unlock at 02:00 UTC, which is 7:00 PM PDT on October 28.

Confirmed Date and Time

The official PlayStation game page and store listing show October 29, 2026 at 02:00 UTC. The Steam listing confirms the Windows PC version and may display October 28 depending on the visitor's region and timezone.

Why Some Pages Still Say September 9

September 9, 2026 was the earlier announced date. The launch later moved to October 29, so older articles, snippets, videos, and cached search results may still show 9/9. Use the current storefront date when planning time off or a purchase.

Platforms at Launch

PS5 and Windows PC are confirmed. No Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, or PS4 version has been announced. See the platform hub for current store links and the PS5 exclusivity explanation.

Preorder, Editions, and Preload

As of July 17, the official US store pages allow wishlisting but do not yet show a purchasable edition. Prices, bonuses, preload timing, and any early-access rules remain unconfirmed. Check the preorder status, editions guide, and preload guide before paying a third-party retailer.

Launch Checklist

  • Confirm the final unlock time in your own PlayStation or Steam region.
  • Check the download size and preload window after they appear officially.
  • Verify final PC requirements before upgrading hardware.
  • Recheck edition contents and refund terms at the point of purchase.
