Important Regional Caveat

These are mathematical conversions of the US PlayStation listing, not a promise that every regional PS Store or Steam account unlocks simultaneously. Storefronts can localize dates, use regional schedules, or update countdowns before launch.

Why Steam May Show October 28

Steam displays dates according to store configuration and visitor locale. A date difference does not automatically mean PC receives early access. Check the live countdown and purchase page near launch.
